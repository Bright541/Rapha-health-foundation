Welcome to Rapha Health Foundation

Founded by Mrs. Adowa Sarpong, a nurse dedicated to extending healthcare services to underserved communities, we provide essential health services, including disease management and regular checkups for nearby villages. Our main branch is located in Abuakwa, and we operate various branches across Ghana.

Visit our main location in Atwima-Mim, where you can find more about our services.

Our Services

The SafeSound Project

Aims to reduce Noise-Induced Hearing Loss (NIHL) through education and free protective devices.

ENT Wellness Check

Free screenings and medications for ear, nose, and throat health issues in vulnerable groups.

The HealthyChoice Initiative

Promotes wellness through education on nutrition, exercise, and mental well-being.

Our Branches in Ghana

We have established several branches across Ghana to better serve our communities:

  • Accra Branch: Focused on urban health initiatives and community outreach.
  • Kumasi Branch: Specializes in maternal and child health programs.
  • Tamale Branch: Offers health education and preventive services in northern regions.
  • Takoradi Branch: Addresses health issues specific to coastal communities.
